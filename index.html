VISUAL IDENTITY

CONCEPT

Logotype consists of three elements: letter K, confluence and rays. River confluence is subtly portrayed in the mark since it is one of the main symbols of Kaunas city. Impression of empathy and warmth is expressed via harmonic color and font combination. Ray-like clear space symbolizes openness of the city, a warm welcome to visit and get to know Kaunas better.
